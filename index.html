Company Description

R & R Sisters Solutions is a dynamic community-based organization committed to providing transformative services for justice-involved individuals, at-risk teens, and others seeking personal development. We serve diverse populations including inmates, court-ordered individuals, domestic violence offenders, and youth. Our programs are rooted in trauma-informed care, cognitive-behavioral interventions, and culturally responsive practices.

Mission Statement

To empower individuals through education, behavior change programs, and personal development resources that foster accountability, self-awareness, and positive transformation.

Objectives

  • Reduce the recidivism rate through evidence-based programs such as Thinking for a Change (T4C) and Batterer’s Intervention Program (BIP)
  • Equip at-risk teens with tools for emotional regulation, goal setting, and healthy decision-making
  • Support courts and community partners with reliable, compliant programming
  • Expand community engagement and advocacy efforts
  • Create a sustainable, scalable model for service delivery

Services Offered

Thinking for Change (T4C)

  • Cognitive-behavioral program focused on decision-making and behavior change
  • Format: In-Person or Live Online
  • Schedule: 25 lessons over 5 weeks (3 sessions/week, 2.5 hours each)
  • Total Hours: 62.5
  • Topics: Social skills, problem-solving, cognitive self-change
  • Includes: Workbook, certificate upon completion
  • Pricing: Online $280 (or 2 payments of $140); In-Person $275 or $250 paid in full

Batterer’s Intervention Program (BIP)

  • Accountability-based program for domestic violence cases
  • Format: In-Person or Online
  • 13 sessions @ 2 hours each (26 hours)
  • Topics: Accountability, abuse cycles, power and control, communication
  • Pricing: Online $240, In-Person $275

Vision Workshops (Youth)

  • Goal-setting and dream-building for youth
  • Format: In-Person or Online
  • 3 Saturdays/month, 4 hours per session
  • Topics: Self-awareness, vision mapping, leadership, planning
  • Pricing: In-Person $60/session or $180/month; Online $50/session or $150/month

Re-entry Support Services

  • One-on-one sessions for resume building, job readiness
  • 90-min session: In-Person $50, Online $40
  • Includes: Resume creation, tips, and 15-min coaching
